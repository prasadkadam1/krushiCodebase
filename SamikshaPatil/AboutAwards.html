June 2022 - Arrell Award Ceremony

Access Agriculture received the 2022 Arrell Global Food Innovation Award in Community Engagement Innovation in Toronto, Canada.

"Local indigenous languages and knowledge are at the core of what Access Agriculture does." - Josephine Rodgers, Executive Director

This award recognizes sharing knowledge to strengthen farmers and communities for healthier, resilient food systems.

October 2021 - International Innovation Award

Access Agriculture won the International Innovation Award for Sustainable Food and Agriculture for empowering youth in sustainable food systems.

Their youth-led model uses solar-powered projectors to deliver agroecology training videos to remote communities.

October 2018 - Prize D4D

Access Agriculture won the Prize Digital for Development in the 'iStandOut' category for its innovative digital approach in using training videos for farmers globally.

October 2016 - EVCOM Clarion Awards

Access Agriculture received the Gold award in 'Innovation' and was highly commended in 'Learning and Education' for its effective video training initiatives.
