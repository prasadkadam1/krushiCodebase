Information on Audio Files

Download and use our free MP3 audio files to share sustainable agricultural practices within your community.

Ideas for Using Audio Files

  • Play programmes at suitable seasonal times to remind your audience about good practices.
  • Invite farmers or guests from local extension services or NGOs for discussion.
  • Host phone-in sessions on subjects raised in the programmes.
  • Be seen and involved in your community through these broadcasts.
  • Show videos at your office or stalls to promote your station.
  • Let us know your innovative use stories for a feature on our website.
