About Access Agriculture

Access Agriculture offers a platform with over 6,000 agroecology training videos in 110 languages to support sustainable agriculture in developing countries. It aims to empower farmers by providing free, downloadable high-quality videos, impacting over 90 million viewers worldwide.

Platform Features

  • Resources for teachers, NGOs, and entrepreneurs
  • Links for impact stories to inspire usage in the field

How To Use

Register on the platform for free to download videos. Search for videos using category, keywords, or language. Videos can be used for farmer training, community screenings, and educational purposes, respecting copyright rules. For translation and re-use in edited content, contact Access Agriculture for permissions.
