Background

Women and young people are the backbone of agricultural development. Promoting agriculture as a business helps create economic value and encourages youth participation in agriculture. Access Agriculture supports the distribution of quality training videos for farmers across the Global South, enabling peer-to-peer learning and fostering organic agriculture adoption through ICT.

Impact

90,000,000 viewers reached

320 entrepreneurs in 18 countries supported

6,000 agroecology videos in 110 languages available

Digisoft Smart Projectors

The smart projector contains all Access Agriculture videos for offline, off-grid training with a solar panel, battery, and sound system for up to 150 people. It helps entrepreneurs screen videos for farmers even without internet access, supporting local language learning.
