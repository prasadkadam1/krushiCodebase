🧾 Purpose

This platform provides free and open access to agricultural schemes, resources, and government initiatives. It is built to empower farmers and support community outreach.

Currently, all users enjoy full access to information. In future updates, specific roles will unlock tailored features and permissions.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't I need an account right now?

To ensure quick and barrier-free access to government schemes and updates.

Will login be required later?

Yes. When applying or saving data, role-based login will be introduced.

Where is my role information stored?

In your browser’s localStorage only. We do not store it on servers.
