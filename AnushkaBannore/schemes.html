PM-KISAN Yojana

Provides ₹6,000/year to small & marginal farmers directly in three installments.

  • Financial support without middlemen
  • Helps purchase seeds, manure, and basic needs
  • Encourages financial inclusion

Fasal Bima Yojana

Insurance cover against crop failure due to natural calamities and diseases.

  • Affordable premium (2% for Kharif, 1.5% Rabi)
  • Protects against major income losses
  • Supports farmer confidence

Soil Health Card

Provides farmers with soil nutrient data and recommended treatment.

  • Better yield with precise input
  • Reduces excessive use of fertilizers
  • Maintains soil quality and fertility

eNAM Platform

Digital agricultural marketing platform to sell produce online.

  • Real-time price discovery
  • Removes middlemen and boosts transparency
  • Pan-India market access

Kisan Credit Card

Credit support to farmers for cultivation and personal expenses.

  • Low interest rates
  • Flexible withdrawal and repayment
  • Helps manage agri expenses efficiently

Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

National-level irrigation scheme to improve water efficiency.

  • Promotes drip/sprinkler systems
  • Ensures water reaches every farm
  • Boosts productivity per drop

RKVY Scheme

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana supports state-level agri innovation and infra.

  • Funds modern agri infrastructure
  • Promotes research, marketing, and storage
  • Flexible state-wise project planning

Sustainable Agriculture Mission

Supports eco-friendly and climate-resilient farming practices.

  • Reduces emissions
  • Promotes organic manure and bio-pesticides
  • Improves crop resistance to climate change

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

Cluster-based organic farming with group certification support.

  • Encourages chemical-free farming
  • Ensures premium pricing in markets
  • Healthier produce and sustainable soil

Agri-Infra Fund

Loan facility for post-harvest infrastructure like cold storage.

  • Reduces wastage
  • Improves supply chain and logistics
  • Enables better price realization

National Beekeeping Mission

Supports scientific beekeeping for increased crop productivity and income.

  • Promotes pollination and biodiversity
  • Income through honey and wax production
  • Training and equipment for beekeepers

Micro Irrigation Fund

Financial aid for micro irrigation projects like drip/sprinklers.

  • Optimizes water usage
  • Improves water-use efficiency
  • Supports high-yield farming

National Food Security Mission

Ensures food security by increasing productivity of key staples.

  • Focus on rice, wheat, pulses, millets
  • Supports farm mechanization
  • Ensures minimum food supply to citizens

Livestock Health & Disease Control

Free vaccination, disease monitoring, and veterinary support to livestock owners.

  • Reduces outbreak risks
  • Increases livestock productivity
  • Supports veterinary infrastructure

Dairy Entrepreneurship Development

Assistance to establish dairy farms, milk storage units, and allied infrastructure.

  • Promotes self-employment in rural areas
  • Ensures better milk handling and quality
  • Improves income of dairy farmers

Agri Export Policy

Policy framework to encourage agri-export and boost farmer income globally.

  • Infrastructure support for export logistics
  • Farmer training on global quality standards
  • Access to international markets
