Annual Reports

The Annual Reports of ICAR provide a detailed account of the organization's achievements, research progress, and outreach activities for each year. These reports serve as a transparent record of ICAR's commitment to advancing Indian agriculture through innovation, collaboration, and capacity building.

2023-24 Highlights

Breakthroughs in crop improvement, climate resilience, and digital agriculture initiatives.

2022-23 Achievements

Major research milestones, new technology releases, and farmer outreach programs.

2021-22 Success Stories

Case studies on successful projects and impact stories from across India.

About the Annual Reports

Each annual report covers research and development highlights, extension and outreach activities, financial and administrative summaries, and future plans. The reports are published online for public access and are used by policymakers, researchers, and stakeholders to track the progress of Indian agriculture.

  • Research: Over 50 new crop varieties released in 2023-24
  • Extension: 1.2 million farmers reached through training and demonstrations
  • Digital Initiatives: Launch of e-Krishi platforms and mobile apps
  • International Collaboration: Joint projects with FAO, IRRI, and CGIAR
  • Financials: Transparent reporting and audited statements available for download

Download the full annual reports or contact reports@icar.gov.in for more information.
